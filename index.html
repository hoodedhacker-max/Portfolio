Who I Am

About Me

My name is Bertin, and I am a digital media student at VVA. Over the course of this program, I have developed a strong foundation in video production, storytelling, and visual communication. Creativity is a gift from God, and I am committed to using it with excellence and purpose.

Through this portfolio, I hope to showcase not only my videos but also the growth in skill behind them. My goal is to use digital media as a platform to inspire, connect, and serve my community.

I am a high school digital media student with a passion for videography. Over the past year, I have put in a lot of work to grow my skills and improve the quality of my videos. What started as something fun quickly became a hobby, and the progress I have made in just one year keeps me motivated to push even further. From learning the basics to producing content I am actually proud of, I am always looking for ways to get better and continue growing as a video editor.

My Testimony

Digital media has given me a platform to tell stories that matter. Through this course, I have learned that creativity is not just a skill it is a calling. God has placed gifts in each of us, and this portfolio is my way of honoring Him by using mine with excellence and purpose.

Beyond creating for myself, I use videography as a way to serve and support others. Whether it is capturing memories, helping others tell their story, or contributing my skills to projects bigger than me, I always look for ways to use what I have learned to make an impact. At the end of the day, it is a hobby I am glad I can share with the people around me.

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters." — Colossians 3:23

Creative & Spiritual Growth

This journey has shaped me academically, creatively, and spiritually. I have grown in confidence, in craft, and in my understanding of what it means to serve others through my gifts. Every video, every edit, every story told was an act of stewardship.

"Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, as faithful stewards of God's grace in its various forms." — 1 Peter 4:10
